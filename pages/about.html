Our story

Built with the belief that dogs deserve a spa experience that's gentle and transparent.

2016—Started in a tiny studio

Two tables, big hearts, lots of happy barks. We focused on calming routines for anxious rescues.

2019—Fear-free certified

Our whole team trained to read body language, slow handling, and reward-first approaches.

2022—Health-first upgrades

Skin-safe product lines, filtered air, and vet-collaboration on coat + paw health check-ins.

Today—Community hub

Workshops on coat care at home, puppy socials, and gentle intro sessions for first-timers.

Happy moments at the studio
