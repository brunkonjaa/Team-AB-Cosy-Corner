About Happy Paw

Welcome to Happy Paws! Here at our prestigious salon we offer the best services to pamper your pooch. We offer stress-free dog grooming for each unique pooch — from a simple wash & trim to a full dental and ear cleaning!

Our staff are highly trained to work with a variety of different breeds and behaviours. Put your trust in us to return a sparkling pooch with a brand new do or a familiar but refreshed doggie.

✨ Every Dog Deserves to Shine ✨

Professional grooming with love and care

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Happy Paw has grown from a small neighborhood grooming shop to one of Dublin's most trusted dog care centers. We believe every dog deserves to look and feel their best!

Our team consists of certified groomers with over 15 years of combined experience. We specialize in breed-specific cuts, creative styling, and most importantly, making sure your furry friend has a tail-wagging good time.
