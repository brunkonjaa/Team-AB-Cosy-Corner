Professional Dog Grooming Services

At Happy Paw, we believe every dog deserves to look and feel their best. Our experienced groomers provide a calm, caring environment and tailor every session to your pet’s individual needs.

Full Groom

A complete nose-to-tail treatment including a warm bath, blow dry, coat styling or clipping, nail trim, ear cleaning, and a finishing spritz. Perfect for maintaining your dog’s coat and confidence.

Bath & Blow Dry

Gentle cleansing with premium shampoos, coat conditioning, and a full blow dry. Keeps your dog’s coat clean, soft, and healthy between grooms.

Mini Groom

A tidy-up between full grooms — includes face, feet, and hygiene trimming, along with a bath and brush-out for a refreshed look.

Puppy’s First Groom

A gentle introduction for pups under 6 months. Short grooming sessions to build comfort and trust with our team.

Deshedding Treatment

Specialized tools remove loose undercoat to reduce shedding and promote a smooth, shiny finish — perfect for double-coated breeds.

Pawdicure & Spa Add-ons

Paw balm, teeth cleaning, tear-stain removal, or a soothing paw massage. Little extras that make a big difference in your dog’s comfort.
